Family Band Website

Previous Next
Details:

This was created as an assignment, but for a real family band. They are located in Northern Utah, and are a band combined of two families. The site was designed and coded by me. The goal was to create a fun, yet professional atmosphere to showcase the band's talents.

Technologies:

HTML5, Bootstrap, SCSS, and Javascript

See Finished Product
Summary:

This project was fun to do because it allowed for a lot of creativity on my part. I loved making the wave audio player (with the assistance of an online javascript library). I also enjoyed playing with fonts and colors to make a fun, dynamic and playful site.

The site relies on the Bootstrap framework, and some customized uses of it.

The main challenge I faced was ensuring the responsiveness of the sight. I used some elements that are not responsive on their own, so it took a bit of SCSS to make things look good on all sizes.
