Out of the Jar - Recipe Blog

Previous Next
Details:

This was created for a CSS course centering on the design process and the Bulma CSS Framework. The target audience were young people who love to bake and have an affinity toward Nutella and Peanut Butter. The goal was to make people want to bake the recipes, and have an effortless experience in finding recipes for easy use.

Technologies:

HTML5, Bulma, CSS3, SASS, Javascript, and Adobe XD

See Wireframe Example
Summary:

This project started with research on what makes a good, effective layout for recipe blogs. The next steps were to create a wireframe and mockup in Adobe XD. After receiving user and teacher feedback that site may benefit from more navigation options on the homepage. After finding a solution that was functional, yet, aesthetic, I moved forward to code the site.

The site relies heavily on Bulmaâ€™s card styling, which gave the sight a fresh, modern look. Having parallax scrolling featured at main images also gave the site a modern look and feel.

The color palette and type was chosen to convey a fun and almost whimsical feeling. After all, it is a site featuring Nutella and other delicious spreads.

The main challenge I faced was ensuring all the images and cards were responsive and looked good on all devices. To achieve this I sized the images to be the same, and implemented media queries to allow for a seamless transition from desktop to mobile.
